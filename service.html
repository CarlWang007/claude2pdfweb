Claude2Pdf - Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 16th, 2023

Registration

To use the Claude2Pdf service, you need to register for an account. During registration, you will need to provide your name, email address, and password. We will protect your personal information and comply with relevant privacy regulations. Please make sure to read and agree to our terms of use when registering.

Login

Once you have registered for a Claude2Pdf account, you can log in using your email address and password. After logging in, you can use our service to generate PDF documents.

Cookies Usage

We use cookies to make our service work properly. These cookies are used to store your login information and other information related to your account. We do not use cookies to collect your personal information or share it with third parties. If you do not want to use cookies, you can disable them in your browser settings.

Payment

We offer subscription-based payment services, and you can choose different packages according to your needs. We accept multiple payment methods, including credit cards and Google Pay. All payment information will be strictly confidential, and we will take necessary security measures to protect your payment information.

Refund Policy

If you are not satisfied with our service for any reason, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchase. We will refund your payment in full, excluding any handling fees. Please note that we will not issue refunds if you request them after the refund deadline.
